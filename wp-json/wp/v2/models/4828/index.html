NZ Handwriting Style<\/em> is published by Australian School Fonts, an online resource and repository of original typefaces for handwriting instruction designed following official guidelines issued by the governments of New Zealand, and of different regions in Australia. It is run by Kevin G Brown, as Graphitype.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

It comprises three styles \u2014 NZ Basic Script GT<\/em>, NZ PreCursive GT<\/em> and NZ Cursive GT Pro<\/em>. They are an unjoined style, a transitional one with entry and exit strokes, and a cursive style respectively.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
