In 2023, the National Council for Educational Research and Training (NCERT) published a new textbook series for English called Mridang<\/em>. The book for grade 1 teaches students simplified print-style letterforms, and no cursive writing instruction is included in grade 1 or 2. While the book acknowledges the graphic designers, illustrators and artists involved in its development, no information is provided about the source of the letterform samples.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
