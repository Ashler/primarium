Victorian Modern Cursive<\/em> is used in Victoria (VIC), Northern Territory (NT) and some parts of Western Australia (WA). The typeface family is published by the Victoria State Government of Education and Training, and is based on and introduced in their own publication, The Teaching of Handwriting<\/em>, which was first published in 1985, and revised in 2002.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
