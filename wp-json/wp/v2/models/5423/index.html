Italica<\/em>, developed by Monica Dengo and typeface designers Riccardo Olocco and Daniele Capo in 2014, is a digital typeface used for handwriting instruction in Italy. It is the result of Scrittura Corsiva<\/em>, Dengo\u2019s project to teach italic style handwriting to elementary school students at the Instituto Comprensivo Giovanni XXIII in Terranuova Bracciolini, Arezzo. The initiative, which began in 2008, led to the institute funding the development of Italica<\/em>.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
