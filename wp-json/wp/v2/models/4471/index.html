Pretoria-based company Edu-fonts PTY LTD publishes two suites of typefaces for handwriting instruction in primary schools. Edu-Aid Grade 1<\/em> features unjoined geometric letters for Grade 1 students, while Edu-Aid Cursive<\/em> is for teaching from Grade 2 onwards. Both typefaces were designed in 2011 by Andr\u00e9 Trollip, following the requirements outlined by Karen Engelbrecht, a primary school teacher and Head of Department Foundation Phase at Loreto School Queenswood in Pretoria.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

These fonts are used by South Africa\u2019s most important educational publishers and content providers including Funda Wande, and E-Classroom, as well as teachers who make and sell teaching aids through their own blogs. Other popular publication, The Happy Handwriter\u2019s cursive book uses a similar font from an unknown author: ABC Cursive.<\/em><\/p>\n\n\n\n
