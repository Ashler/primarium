Schools in New South Wales (NSW) and the Australian Capital Territory (ACT) follow the Foundation Approach<\/em> to teaching handwriting, which was created as a result of research involving students, staff and parents from forty schools in the region.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

It consists of Foundation Handwriting Movements<\/em>, a set of three fine-motor movements, and NSW Foundation Handwriting Style<\/em>. NSW Foundation Handwriting Style<\/em> comprises two progressive stages: unjoined letters (known as \u201cmanuscript handwriting\u201d in NSW), followed by a semi-joined cursive style.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
