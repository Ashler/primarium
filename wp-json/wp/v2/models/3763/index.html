In 1981, Christian Clemens created a series of handwriting textbooks called Bedre h\u00e5ndskrivning<\/em>, which are currently published and distributed by the publisher Alinea. According to the methods that are outlined in them, students learn Trykbogstaver<\/em>, or simplified print letters in first and second grade, and then move to a cursive hand, which is locally known as Grundskrift,<\/em> or basic script.<\/em><\/p>\n\n\n\n
