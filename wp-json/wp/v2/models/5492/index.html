Originating in the UK, Twinkl Educational Publishing is a globally operating company that provides educational material and support resources for schools. In 2016, they identified the opportunity to develop their own model for teaching handwriting, which would meet the guidelines of the 2014 English curriculum. To this end, they hired TypeTogether to develop Twinkl Sans, a custom font for the composition of texts for children’s reading, and in 2017, a cursive font project, based on Twinkl Sans, was initiated. Twinkl Cursive was made for designing resources for teaching handwriting, and usage by teachers for the preparation of literacy materials.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
