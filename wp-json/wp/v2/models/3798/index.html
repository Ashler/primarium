Brigitta Falk\u2019s book series Handskrift<\/em> (2010) features stavskrift<\/em>, the cursive style developed by Jakob Rask Arnesen (1918-2008) and Sigrun Nygaard Moriggi in 1973, in a new typeface family called Norsk Stavskrift<\/em> designed by Ida Nygaard in 2010. Both Arnesen and Moriggi\u2019s original work, and this typeface family were developed for the book publisher H. Aschehoug & Co.<\/p>\n\n\n

In first grade, uppercase and lowercase trykkskrift<\/em>, or simplified print is taught; it has unconnected letters. In the middle of the second year or beginning of the third, students learn stavskrift,<\/em> which has joining strokes between letters.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
