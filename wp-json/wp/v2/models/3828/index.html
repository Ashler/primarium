Editorial Barcanova was founded in 1980, and it specialises in educational publications; resources for schools, teachers, and students; and children\u2019s and adult literature. Its activities have been focused on Catalonia’s cultural and educational landscape.<\/p>\n\n\n

The typeface used in Editorial Barcanova\u2019s handwriting textbooks is MeMimas<\/em>, developed in 1991 by Jos\u00e9 Manuel Ur\u00f3s of Type-\u00d8-Tones. According to Ur\u00f3s, a basic version of MeMimas<\/em> was also acquired by the Generalitat de Catalunya<\/em>, or the government of Catalonia, to be made available to schools and educators in the autonomous province during the 1990s. An updated version of the design, called MeMimas Pro<\/em>, was released in 2016, and it offers a new set of ornate cursive capitals, additional weights (medium, bold and black), as well as slanted and dotted styles.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

In general, MeMimas<\/em> reproduces the Spanish vertical cursive writing approach with some modifications, such as a lower x-height, tall extensions, the letter z with descending loop, and a set of cursive numerals.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
